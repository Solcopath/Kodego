Beginner's Guide

Straight from the files of Brimstone, this is the VALORANT Beginner's Guide. Whether this is your first rotation or you're needing a refresher, we figure it's best you take it from the Agent who's seen it all before.

Below is what to know before your first game. Pay attention. Might learn something.

What is Valorant?

Before you enter your first round, you get to pick from a group of Agents, each with their own role and special skills designed to work alongside each other toward a win. You can dig into the specifics of each Agent on our Agents page.

Choose a Role

After everyone selects their Agent, you'll jump into a random map and enter the first round. You'll either attacker or defender and switch sides in between. You'll need to navigate the buy phase, which lets you purchase weapons and abilities.

What's in a Round?

There's a gun for every situation and you can comb the details of each weapon on our dedicated arsenal page. When in doubt, try buying based on a weapon's type and see how it fits your style, expertise, and your team's strategy.
